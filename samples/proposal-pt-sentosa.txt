TECHNICAL PROPOSAL
MANAGED SECURITY OPERATIONS CENTER SERVICES FOR PT SENTOSA ABADI FINANSIAL

Prepared for: PT Sentosa Abadi Finansial
Date: 22 May 2026
Reference: NMS-PROP-2026-0518

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Nimbus Secure is pleased to submit this proposal to operate a 24/7 Managed Security
Operations Center for PT Sentosa Abadi Finansial. As a financial technology
operator supervised by OJK, Sentosa requires continuous protection of its
lending platform and mobile application against an evolving threat landscape.

Nimbus Secure proposes a fully managed SOC service combining a cloud-native SIEM,
an experienced analyst team operating around the clock, and a structured
incident-response capability. Our service is designed to give Sentosa
measurable risk reduction, regulator-ready reporting, and a predictable
operational security posture. We have delivered comparable services to other
regulated Indonesian enterprises and operate entirely from Indonesian
data-center facilities.

We are confident that this proposal meets Sentosa's objectives and look forward
to the opportunity to partner with your team.

2. COMPANY PROFILE

Nimbus Secure is an Indonesian managed-services provider established in 2011,
specializing in managed cloud, disaster recovery, and managed security
services. Nimbus Secure operates from Tier III data-center facilities located in
Jakarta and Surabaya, all within Indonesian jurisdiction.

Nimbus Secure holds ISO/IEC 27001:2022 certification for its information security
management system and is staffed by analysts and engineers holding industry
certifications including CISSP, OSCP, GCIH, and CEH. Nimbus Secure currently protects
production environments for clients in banking, insurance, government, and
e-commerce.

3. UNDERSTANDING OF REQUIREMENTS AND SCOPE OF WORK

Nimbus Secure has reviewed the Terms of Reference and understands that Sentosa
requires continuous monitoring, detection, and response for its production
environment. Our proposed scope of work is as follows.

3.1 24/7 Security Monitoring
Nimbus Secure will provide round-the-clock monitoring of Sentosa's cloud and
on-premise production systems from our staffed Security Operations Center.
Monitoring covers network, endpoint, application, and identity telemetry, with
coverage 24 hours a day, every day of the year.

3.2 SIEM Deployment and Management
Nimbus Secure will deploy and operate a cloud-native SIEM platform. We will onboard
log sources across Sentosa's in-scope systems, develop correlation rules tuned
to the financial-services threat model, and continuously refine detection
content to reduce false positives.

3.3 Incident Response
Nimbus Secure operates a documented incident-response process aligned with NIST
guidance. Our analysts triage alerts, classify incident severity, and
coordinate containment and recovery. For CRITICAL incidents, Nimbus Secure commits to
acknowledge within 15 minutes and to begin containment within 30 minutes, at
any time of day.

3.4 Reporting
Nimbus Secure will provide a monthly executive security report summarizing incidents,
trends, and posture, and will hold a quarterly service-review meeting with
Sentosa management.

3.5 Threat Intelligence
Nimbus Secure integrates commercial and community threat-intelligence feeds into its
monitoring platform, enriching detection with current indicators of compromise.

3.6 Data Residency
All Sentosa log and security data will be stored and processed exclusively
within Nimbus Secure's Indonesian data-center facilities. No customer or security
data will leave Indonesian jurisdiction.

3.7 Compliance Alignment
Nimbus Secure's SOC operates under an ISO/IEC 27001-certified management system, and
our reporting is designed to support Sentosa's information-security governance
obligations.

4. SERVICE DELIVERY METHODOLOGY

Nimbus Secure follows a four-phase delivery methodology: Assess, Onboard, Operate, and
Improve. During Assess, we review Sentosa's environment and define in-scope
assets. During Onboard, we connect log sources and validate detection coverage.
During Operate, the SOC runs continuous monitoring and response. During Improve,
we tune detections and review service performance with Sentosa.

Our analysts work in three shifts to maintain unbroken coverage, supported by a
senior incident-response lead and a detection-engineering function that
maintains and improves correlation content.

5. PROJECT TIMELINE

Nimbus Secure will transition the service in phases. We will begin with an assessment
of Sentosa's environment, followed by onboarding of log sources and validation
of monitoring coverage, after which the service will move into full steady-state
operation. Nimbus Secure will work closely with Sentosa to complete the transition in
a timely manner and will agree the detailed schedule during contract
finalization.

6. PROPOSED TEAM

Nimbus Secure will assign a dedicated delivery team to the Sentosa account:

  - Service Delivery Manager — single point of accountability for the service.
  - SOC Lead — supervises analyst shifts and detection quality.
  - Incident Response Lead — owns major-incident coordination.
  - Security Analysts (Tier 1 and Tier 2) — staffed across three shifts.
  - Detection Engineer — maintains SIEM correlation content.

Key personnel hold CISSP, OSCP, and GCIH certifications. Curricula vitae of key
personnel are available on request.

7. RELEVANT EXPERIENCE AND REFERENCES

Nimbus Secure has delivered managed security services since 2018, giving more than
seven years of operational experience in this domain. Representative engagements
include managed SOC services for a national multi-finance company and security
monitoring for a regional commercial bank. Client references can be provided to
Sentosa during the evaluation process.

8. SERVICE LEVELS

Nimbus Secure commits to the incident-response service levels described in section
3.3. Detailed service-level definitions for monitoring availability and
